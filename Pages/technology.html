LAUNCH VECHICLE

A launch vechicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled vechicle used to carry payload from earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our WEB-X carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation. Standing 150 metres tall, it's quite an awe-inspiring sight on the launch pad
